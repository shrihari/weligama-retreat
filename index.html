Activities

We will be joined by a licensed UK psychologist, an Ayurvedic doctor, and a yoga instructor. We will combine Vedic principles and modern psychology to focus on mental, physical, and spiritual health.

  • Deep dives into health goals
  • 1:1 and group psychotherapy
  • 1:1 coaching sessions
  • Meditation & Yoga
  • Nutrition talks
  • Ayurvedic treatments and massages
  • Hike and walk-in rainforest

About your host

Hi! I am Guni! my passion for health and wellness has been a driving force in my life. It began with my personal health journey and has since inspired many of my career choices. I founded a complementary healthcare application called dotiv, host a lifestyle medicine podcast (@dotivthepodcast), and currently work for the World Health Organisation. My goal is to empower others to prioritize their health and well-being, and this retreat is a perfect opportunity to do just that.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can attend this wellness retreat?

This retreat is open to men and women of all ages who are interested in prioritizing their health and well-being.

Where is the retreat located?

The retreat is located in Weligama, Southern Province of Sri Lanka, in the Bougainvillea Boutique Villa in Sri Lanka.

How long is the retreat?

The retreat is a 5-day, 4-night experience.

What is the cost of the retreat?

The cost of the retreat varies depending on the package you choose. We offer an early bird discount, and more information can be found on the website.

What is the daily schedule like during the retreat?

Each day will include a variety of activities to support your mental, physical, and spiritual health. Activities will include deep dives on your health goals, 1:1 and group psychotherapy, 1:1 coaching sessions, meditation on the beach, yoga on the terrace, nutrition talks, Ayurvedic treatments and massages, and a walk in the rainforest of the southern province of Sri Lanka.

Will there be free time during the retreat?

Yes, there will be some free time each day for you to relax and enjoy the beautiful surroundings.

Is the retreat safe?

Yes, the retreat is located in a safe and secure property, and we will take all necessary precautions to ensure your safety and well-being during your stay.

What is the cancellation policy?

You can cancel anytime two months prior your the retreat. If you cancel two months before the retreat you will be reimbursed 25%.

How many participants are there in each retreat?

We limit the number of participants in each retreat to ensure a personalized and intimate experience. The maximum number of participants is 10.

Can I bring a friend or family member with me?

Yes, you are welcome to bring a friend or family member with you. Please note that there will be an additional cost for any additional attendees.
